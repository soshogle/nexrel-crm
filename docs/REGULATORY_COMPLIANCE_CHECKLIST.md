# Regulatory Compliance Checklist

## For Dental Practice Management Software with DICOM

---

## ✅ What You Already Have

- ✅ **Law 25 Compliance** (Canadian data residency)
- ✅ **Privacy Compliance** (encryption, consent)
- ✅ **Data Security** (Canadian storage)

---

## ⚠️ What You May Need

### 1. Medical Device Determination

**Action:** Get legal/regulatory opinion

**Questions to Answer:**
- [ ] Do you claim diagnostic capabilities?
- [ ] Do you make treatment recommendations?
- [ ] Do you market as diagnostic tool?
- [ ] Does software replace professional judgment?

**If NO to all:** Likely NOT a medical device ✅

**If YES to any:** May need registration ⚠️

**Cost:** $2,000-5,000 for consultation

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

---

### 2. Business License

**Action:** Register your business

**Required:**
- [ ] Business registration (federal/provincial)
- [ ] Tax registration
- [ ] Professional services license (if applicable)

**Cost:** $100-500

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

---

### 3. Professional Liability Insurance

**Action:** Get insurance coverage

**Recommended Coverage:**
- [ ] Professional liability ($1-5M)
- [ ] Errors & omissions ($1-5M)
- [ ] Cyber liability ($1M+)
- [ ] General liability ($1M+)

**Cost:** $2,000-10,000/year

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

---

### 4. Terms of Service & Disclaimers

**Action:** Create legal documents

**Required Documents:**
- [ ] Terms of Service
- [ ] Privacy Policy ✅ (you have this)
- [ ] User Agreement
- [ ] Software Disclaimers
- [ ] AI Analysis Disclaimer

**Key Disclaimers:**
- "For information purposes only"
- "Not for diagnostic use"
- "Requires professional interpretation"
- "Not a substitute for professional judgment"

**Cost:** $2,000-5,000 (lawyer)

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

---

### 5. Medical Device Registration (If Required)

**Only if determined to be a medical device:**

- [ ] Device classification (Class II/III)
- [ ] Quality Management System (ISO 13485)
- [ ] Technical documentation
- [ ] Clinical evidence
- [ ] Health Canada submission
- [ ] Post-market surveillance

**Cost:** $50,000-200,000+

**Timeline:** 1-2 years

---

## Recommended Path (NOT Medical Device)

### Phase 1: Legal Foundation (1-2 months)

1. **Get regulatory opinion** ($2-5K)
   - Confirm NOT a medical device
   - Get written opinion

2. **Create legal documents** ($2-5K)
   - Terms of Service
   - Disclaimers
   - User agreements

3. **Get insurance** ($2-10K/year)
   - Professional liability
   - Errors & omissions

**Total:** $6-20K, 1-2 months

---

### Phase 2: Business Setup (2-4 weeks)

1. **Business registration** ($100-500)
2. **Tax registration** (free)
3. **Bank account** (varies)

**Total:** $100-500, 2-4 weeks

---

### Phase 3: Compliance (Ongoing)

1. **Maintain insurance** (annual)
2. **Update legal documents** (as needed)
3. **Monitor regulations** (ongoing)

## If Determined to Be Medical Device

### Additional Requirements:

1. **Quality Management System**
   - ISO 13485 certification
   - Documented processes
   - Quality control

2. **Technical Documentation**
   - Software specifications
   - Risk analysis
   - Validation testing

3. **Clinical Evidence**
   - Clinical studies (if Class III)
   - Literature review
   - Performance data

4. **Regulatory Submission**
   - Health Canada application
   - Review process (6-12 months)
   - Approval/license

5. **Post-Market Surveillance**
   - Adverse event reporting
   - Periodic updates
   - Compliance monitoring

**Total Cost:** $50,000-200,000+
**Timeline:** 1-2 years
