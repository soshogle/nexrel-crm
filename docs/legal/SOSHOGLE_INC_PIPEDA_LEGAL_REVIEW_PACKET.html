1) Purpose and Scope

This legal draft translates the client requirements and PIPEDA accountability guidance into an operational privacy management program for Soshogle Inc. and Nexrel.

It governs collection, use, disclosure, retention, access, correction, complaint handling, and incident response for personal information handled through Nexrel.

2) Governance and Accountability

  • Appoint and publicly identify a Privacy Officer with authority to intervene on privacy issues.
  • Maintain senior-management support and governance oversight for privacy controls.
  • Maintain a documented privacy management program with periodic review and remediation.

3) Consent, Purpose Limitation, and Transparency

  • Identify and document purposes for each category of personal information collected.
  • Provide clear purpose notice before or at collection.
  • Obtain meaningful consent for collection/use/disclosure where required.
  • Obtain new consent for materially new purposes.
  • Enable consent withdrawal (subject to legal/contractual constraints) and explain implications.

4) Retention, Accuracy, Access, and Complaints

  • Maintain retention/destruction timetables by data category and legal hold requirements.
  • Maintain procedures for correction and dispute notation where data accuracy is challenged.
  • Respond to access requests within 30 days, with lawful extension handling and notice.
  • Provide complaint intake, impartial review, tracked outcomes, and corrective action records.

5) Technical Security and Safety Measures in Nexrel

The following controls are currently implemented in the codebase and intended to support protection of customer and owner data.

Control Domain Implemented Measure Reference
Encryption in Transit HTTPS security posture via strict headers, HSTS, CSP, and production redirect from HTTP to HTTPS. `middleware.ts`
Encryption at Rest (App-Level) AES-256-GCM encryption helper for sensitive values; production now fails closed when encryption key material is missing. `lib/encryption.ts`
Field-Level Secret Protection Helper utilities for encrypting sensitive DB fields (API keys/tokens/secrets) with encrypted prefix handling. `lib/crypto-fields.ts`
Document Security Application-level encryption for stored documents plus server-side encryption metadata for storage backends. `lib/storage/canadian-storage-service.ts`
Access and Incident Traceability Audit and privacy-related models exist for access logs, access requests, and breach tracking. `prisma/schema.prisma` (`AuditLog`, `DocumentAccessLog`, `DataAccessRequest`, `DataBreach`)
PII Logging Safety Structured logger includes built-in redaction of common PII/token fields. `lib/logger.ts`
Rights Handling Service logic exists for patient access/deletion request workflow handling. `lib/storage/patient-rights-service.ts`

Encryption Statement (Counsel Review Required)

Nexrel applies encryption in transit and encryption at rest for sensitive data paths. For legal materials, describe this as "encryption in transit and at rest with key management and access controls" unless a specific workflow is architected as strict cryptographic end-to-end encryption.

6) Third-Party Processor and Cross-Border Requirements

  • Maintain documented processor inventory and data flow map.
  • Use contractual terms requiring equivalent safeguards and purpose limitation.
  • Assess transfer risk and provide customer transparency on jurisdictional access risk.

7) Breach/Incident Protocol

  • Document incident intake, severity classification, containment, and remediation.
  • Document notification decisioning and legal notice pathways.
  • Maintain post-incident corrective action and control hardening records.
