# SOP-005 - Privacy Incident and Breach Response

Document ID: SOP-005  
Owner: Privacy Officer + Security Lead  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual and post-incident

## 1. Purpose

Provide a consistent process to detect, triage, contain, investigate, notify, and remediate privacy incidents and breaches.

## 2. Severity Levels

- Low: limited exposure, low sensitivity, no evidence of misuse
- Medium: potential impact requiring coordinated response
- High/Critical: sensitive data, broad impact, or strong misuse risk

## 3. Procedure

1. Intake incident report and assign incident ID.
2. Triage severity and activate response team.
3. Contain and preserve evidence.
4. Assess scope, affected records, and risk of significant harm.
5. Determine legal notification requirements with counsel.
6. Execute required notifications.
7. Implement remediation and verify closure.
8. Complete post-incident review with corrective actions.

## 4. Required Records

- Timeline of events
- Affected systems/data categories
- Notification decisions and communications
- Corrective actions and closure evidence
