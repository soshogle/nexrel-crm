# SOP-002 - Consent Management

Document ID: SOP-002  
Owner: Privacy Officer + Product Owner  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual

## 1. Purpose

Ensure meaningful, valid, and documented consent for collection/use/disclosure of personal information where required by PIPEDA.

## 2. Procedure

1. Define purpose(s) before collection.
2. Present clear consent notice at or before collection.
3. Capture consent event (who, when, method, version).
4. Enforce purpose-limited use/disclosure.
5. Trigger re-consent for new material purposes.
6. Provide consent withdrawal channel and explain effects.

## 3. Consent Record Minimum Fields

- Data subject identifier
- Consent statement version
- Timestamp and timezone
- Collection channel (web/app/phone/document)
- Purpose(s) accepted
- Withdrawal status and timestamp (if applicable)

## 4. Controls

- Consent language readability review
- Versioned consent text archive
- Audit trail for consent changes

## 5. SLA

- Consent withdrawal requests reflected in systems within 5 business days unless legal/contractual exception applies.
