# SOP-008 - Privacy Internal Audit and Annual Review

Document ID: SOP-008  
Owner: Privacy Officer  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual

## 1. Purpose

Assess privacy program effectiveness, identify gaps, and ensure continuous compliance improvement.

## 2. Audit Scope

- Governance/accountability records
- Consent records and versioning
- Access/deletion request SLA performance
- Incident register and post-incident actions
- Retention/destruction evidence
- Vendor and cross-border controls
- Technical controls sampling

## 3. Procedure

1. Issue annual audit plan and criteria.
2. Sample control evidence and validate operation.
3. Record findings by severity.
4. Assign corrective actions with due dates and owners.
5. Present annual review report to leadership and legal.

## 4. Deliverables

- Annual Privacy Audit Report
- Risk Register updates
- Corrective Action Plan
- Policy/SOP revision recommendations
