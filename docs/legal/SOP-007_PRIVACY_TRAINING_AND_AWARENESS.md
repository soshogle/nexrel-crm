# SOP-007 - Privacy Training and Awareness

Document ID: SOP-007  
Owner: Privacy Officer + HR/People Ops  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual

## 1. Purpose

Ensure all personnel understand privacy obligations, consent handling, data request processes, and incident escalation.

## 2. Audience

- All employees
- Contractors with personal information access
- Role-specific modules for engineering, support, and operations

## 3. Training Requirements

- New hire privacy onboarding within 30 days.
- Annual refresher completion for all applicable personnel.
- Triggered training after policy or legal changes.

## 4. Curriculum Minimums

- PIPEDA principles and accountability
- Meaningful consent
- Access/correction/deletion request handling
- Logging hygiene and PII redaction
- Incident/breach reporting responsibilities

## 5. Evidence

- Completion records
- Quiz/assessment scores
- Exception/non-completion escalations
