# SOP-006 - Third-Party and Cross-Border Processing

Document ID: SOP-006  
Owner: Privacy Officer + Legal + Procurement  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual and on vendor change

## 1. Purpose

Ensure third-party processors provide comparable privacy protection and that cross-border processing is risk-assessed and transparent.

## 2. Procedure

1. Maintain vendor register with data categories and purpose.
2. Perform privacy/security due diligence before onboarding.
3. Execute contractual terms for:
   - purpose limitation,
   - confidentiality,
   - safeguard standards,
   - subprocessor controls,
   - incident notification,
   - return/deletion obligations.
4. Document jurisdictional processing and customer transparency statements.
5. Reassess high-risk vendors annually.

## 3. Required Evidence

- Vendor assessments
- Signed DPAs/contracts
- Subprocessor list and updates
- Cross-border risk assessments
