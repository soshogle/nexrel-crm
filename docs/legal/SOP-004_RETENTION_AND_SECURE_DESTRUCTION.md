# SOP-004 - Retention and Secure Destruction

Document ID: SOP-004  
Owner: Privacy Officer + Engineering/Security  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual

## 1. Purpose

Ensure personal information is retained only as long as necessary for identified purposes and legal obligations, then securely destroyed.

## 2. Procedure

1. Classify data category and applicable retention requirement.
2. Define retention period in ROPA/retention schedule.
3. Enforce legal hold overrides when required.
4. Execute secure destruction on expiry.
5. Record destruction evidence and exceptions.

## 3. Destruction Standards

- Database records: secure delete/soft-delete policy per legal requirement.
- Files/objects: provider deletion + application-level reference cleanup.
- Backup/media: destruction aligned to provider lifecycle and contractual controls.

## 4. Evidence

- Retention schedule revision history
- Deletion job logs
- Exception and legal hold register
