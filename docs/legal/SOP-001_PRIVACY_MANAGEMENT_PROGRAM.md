# SOP-001 - Privacy Management Program

Document ID: SOP-001  
Owner: Privacy Officer  
Applies to: Soshogle Inc. and Nexrel  
Effective Date: ****\_\_****  
Review Cycle: Annual (or upon material change)

## 1. Purpose

Establish and operate a privacy management program aligned with PIPEDA fair information principles, with clear accountability, governance, and demonstrable controls.

## 2. Scope

All employees, contractors, systems, products, and service providers handling personal information under Soshogle/Nexrel control.

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities

- Privacy Officer: program owner, escalation authority, legal liaison.
- Engineering/Security: technical safeguards, change control, logging.
- Product/Operations: consent UX/processes, records, customer communications.
- Legal: policy review, contractual and jurisdictional validation.

## 4. Procedure

1. Maintain current privacy policy set and SOP set.
2. Maintain record of processing activities (ROPA) and data inventory.
3. Run privacy impact/risk assessment for new initiatives and major changes.
4. Verify consent, retention, access, and incident procedures are active.
5. Conduct periodic control testing and remediation tracking.
6. Report compliance posture to senior management quarterly.

## 5. Required Evidence

- Approved policy/SOP versions
- ROPA register updates
- Risk assessment records
- Training completion logs
- Incident and request handling logs
- Annual review report and corrective actions

## 6. KPI / SLA Targets

- 100% of material features have privacy review before release.
- 100% of privacy incidents have documented triage and closure.
- 100% of annual policy reviews completed on time.

## 7. Non-Compliance Handling

Any deviation must be logged, risk-rated, and remediated with owner and deadline.
